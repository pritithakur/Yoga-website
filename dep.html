Yoga for depression

Yoga is a gentle exercise that incorporates both meditation and controlled, physical movements. The focus on deep breathing and stretching your body is effective for relieving the symptoms of depression, such as sleep troubles, pain, and a loss of energy. Try and practice these poses along with few breathing exercises and you'll be good to go!

1. Ardha Setu Bandhasana
2. Baddha Konasana
3. Dhanurasana
4. Bhujangasana
5. Malasana
