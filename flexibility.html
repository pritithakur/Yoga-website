Yoga for Flexibility

Increasing flexibility helps in greater range of motion, less muscle tension, better posture, lower risk of injuries, less stress and improved circulation. These poses will help increase your flexibility in back, core, hip, shoulder and neck.

1. Parsvottanasana
2. Janu Sirasana
3. Dhanurasana
4. Anjaneyasana
