Yoga for Digestion

Digestive issues can be a serious problem for some people.These issues can range from mild to debilitating depending on the person. Thankfully, there are several yoga poses for digestion aid and overall wellness. Yoga postures, or asanas, can help you care for the health of your colon and prevent future issues from appearing.

1. Matsyendrasana
2. Apanasana
3. Setu Bandha Sarvangasana
4. Bhujangasana
5. Vajrasana
